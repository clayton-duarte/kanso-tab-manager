# Privacy Policy

**Kanso Tab Manager** is committed to protecting your privacy. This document explains what data the extension collects, how it's used, and your rights.

## Summary

- **No data collection by us** — We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data to our servers
- **No analytics or tracking** — We don't use any analytics, telemetry, or tracking services
- **No third-party sharing** — Your data is never shared with third parties
- **Your data stays yours** — All data is stored locally or in your own GitHub Gist

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

The extension stores the following data locally in your browser using Chrome's Storage API:

- **Profiles and Workspaces** — Your custom profiles, workspaces, and links
- **User Preferences** — Settings like accent color and profile order
- **GitHub Personal Access Token** — If you enable Gist sync (stored securely in local storage)

This data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly enable Gist sync.

### GitHub Gist Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable sync:

- Your profiles and workspaces are saved to **your own GitHub Gist**
- The extension uses your GitHub Personal Access Token to authenticate
- Data is transmitted directly between your browser and GitHub's API
- We never see or have access to your token or synced data

## Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `storage` | Store your profiles, workspaces, and preferences locally |
| `tabs` | Switch tabs when changing workspaces, open links, and preserve pinned tabs |

## What We Don't Do

- ❌ Collect browsing history
- ❌ Track your activity
- ❌ Send data to external servers
- ❌ Use analytics or telemetry
- ❌ Share data with third parties
- ❌ Access your data in any way

## Your Rights

- **Delete your data** — Uninstall the extension to remove all local data
- **Export your data** — Your data is stored in a GitHub Gist you control
- **Revoke sync access** — Delete your GitHub token from settings at any time
